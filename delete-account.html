How to Delete Your Account

We understand you may want to delete your PC Gaffer account. You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to jirubalusus@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your account
Send Deletion Request

Option 2: In-App

Within the PC Gaffer app:

  • Go to Settings
  • Select Account
  • Tap Delete Account

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account credentials — your email and password hash
  • Game save data — all your saved games, team data, and progress
  • Leaderboard entries — your scores and rankings
  • Settings & preferences — any customizations you've made

⚠️ Warning: This action is irreversible

Once your account is deleted, all data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Make sure you want to proceed before submitting a deletion request.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at jirubalusus@gmail.com.
