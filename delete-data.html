How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of specific data associated with your PC Gaffer account without deleting your entire account. Use one of the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to jirubalusus@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your account
  • Specify which data you want deleted (see table below)
Send Deletion Request

Option 2: In-App

Within the PC Gaffer app:

  • Go to Settings
  • Select Account
  • Tap Manage Data
  • Choose which data to delete

Types of Data You Can Delete

Data Type Description Impact
Game Saves Your saved games, team data, and progress You'll start fresh; account remains active
Leaderboard Your scores and rankings Removed from all leaderboards
All Data Complete account and all associated data Full account deletion (see Account Deletion)

⚠️ Warning: Data deletion is permanent

Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Game progress, achievements, and rankings will be permanently lost.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Data We Retain

Even after data deletion, we may retain:

  • Account credentials — if you only delete game data (not full account)
  • Anonymized analytics — aggregate usage data that cannot identify you
  • Legal records — data required for legal compliance (if applicable)

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at jirubalusus@gmail.com.
