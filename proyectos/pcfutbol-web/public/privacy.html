Privacy Policy — PC Gaffer

Last updated: February 2, 2026

FOOTBALL MANAGER

1. Introduction

Welcome to PC Gaffer ("the App"), a football manager simulation game developed by PC Gaffer Team. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Email address — collected during account creation via Firebase Authentication. Used solely for login and account identification.
  • Game save data — your in-game progress, team selections, and settings are stored in Google Firebase Firestore to enable cloud saves and cross-device access.
  • Leaderboard data — game scores and rankings submitted to community leaderboards are stored in Firestore.
  • Basic analytics — we use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns (e.g., page views, session duration). This data is aggregated and anonymous.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information beyond your email address.
  • We do not display advertisements or share data with ad networks.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect location data, contacts, or device identifiers for tracking purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and provide access to your account.
  • To save and restore your game progress across sessions and devices.
  • To maintain community leaderboards and rankings.
  • To improve the App through anonymized usage analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and Firestore database.
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which employ industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time.

10. How to Delete Your Account & Data

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  • Option 1 (Email): Send an email to jirubalusus@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.
  • Option 2 (In-App): Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app.

What gets deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email, password hash)
  • All saved game data
  • Leaderboard entries associated with your account
  • Any other personal data stored in our systems

Timeline: Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
